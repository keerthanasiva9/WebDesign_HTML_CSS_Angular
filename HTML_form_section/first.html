Privacy Act Statement

Apply for new Social Security Card

You must provide at least two documents to prove:

  1. Age
  2. Identity
  3. U.S citizenship or current lawful
  4. Work-Authorized immigration status

NOTE:If you are 12 or older and have never received a Social Security number,you must apply in person.

Replace a Social Security Card

  1. You must provide one document to prove your identity.
  2. If you were born outside the U.S., you must provide documents to prove your U.S. citizenship or current, lawful, work-authorized status

--> Click here to view explanation of acceptable documents

Changing Information on your Social Security Card

You must provide documents to prove:

  • Support the requested change
  • Identity
  • Establish the reason for the change
  • U.S citizenship or current lawful
  • If you were born outside U.S.,provide work-authorized immigration status
  • A document supporting a name change must be recent and identify you by both your old and new names.
  • If the name change event occurred over two years ago or if the name change document does not have enough information to prove your identity.
